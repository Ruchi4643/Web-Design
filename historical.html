Khujraho Temple, one of the famous historical places of Madhya Pradesh, is located in Chhatarpur district of Bundelkhand region of Madhya Pradesh. Khujraho Temple is famous not only across the country but across the world for its ancient and medieval temples. Here is a group of 22 temples of Khajuraho, which remain the center of attraction for travelers from all over the world. The intricate carvings and exquisite erotic sculptures here can hypnotize any history and art lover to visit Khajuraho.

Gwalior Fort is a strong fort built on the top of a huge mountain. It extends for about 3 kilometers. Its structure is the pride of Gwalior. As soon as you reach Gwalior, you can see the beauty of this fort from any nook and corner. Man Mandir, Gujri Mahal, water tanks etc. are present inside this fort.

Sanchi Stupa is 46 km from Bhopal, the capital of Madhya Pradesh state. It is situated on the banks of river Betba in the north-east at a distance of 1000 meters. This place is world famous for its attractive art works, and is one of the famous historical monuments of Madhya Pradesh. A good glimpse of Buddhist art and architecture can be seen in the sculptures and monuments present at this place.

Orchha is a historic city located on the banks of the Betwa River in Madhya Pradesh, known for its grand palaces and intricately carved temples. Famously known as the City of Palaces, it is world famous for the classic murals, frescos and chhatris (cenotaphs) that were built to commemorate the Bundela rulers. The old world charm of Orchha has mesmerized tourists from all over the world.
